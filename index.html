Who is Arno?

「記憶というのは不思議なものだ。あの時の僕は、目の前にある光景にたいして、ほとんど注意を払っていなかった。 それが十八年後の僕に、これほどまでに鮮明なイメージを持って蘇るなんて、まったく考えてもみなかった。 あの時の僕にとって風景はどうでもよかったのだ。僕が考えていたのは自分自身のことだった。 自分がものごとの意味を考える能力を失ってしまったことについてだった。」——村上春樹『ノルウェイの森』

Hi there, this is Arno. I studied Statistics at Michigan State University. Later, life took me to the University of British Columbia, where I finished my Master's in Computational Linguistics.

Memory is a funny thing. When I was in the scene, I hardly paid it any attention. I never stopped to think of it as something that would make a lasting impression — certainly never imagined that 18 years later I would recall it in such detail. I didn't give a damn about the scenery that day. I was thinking about myself. I was thinking about how I'd lost the ability to see meanings in things.

Life's journey is rarely smooth. Toward the end of 2023, I faced the collapse of my own entrepreneurial venture and fell into the lowest point of my life — battling depression, confusion, disrupted routines, and even losing myself in smoking, drinking, and escaping from reality. Yet it was precisely during those dark times that I rediscovered who I was. In 2024, I emerged from the cocoon and was reborn. I was fortunate enough to join a major tech company, where my career flourished. I built healthy relationships, received an offer from my dream school for graduate studies, and, above all, regained the confidence and passion I had long lost. With renewed spirit, I stepped forward into a new chapter of my life.

The road ahead remains full of uncertainty and challenges, yet I continue to forge ahead on this rugged and fascinating path. May we encourage each other along the way — may we face the thorns without fear, hold fast to our dreams, and keep moving forward.
